30Kw Smart Nano Grid, Chotkei, Angul, Odisha 2015 - 2016

Website

A smart Nano Grid comprising of 30 Kw solar power plant and providing supply to 115 homes in Chotkei Village, Odisha. The smart grid is equipped with completely autonomous stem to make it hassle-free to operate. The grid is connected to internet by using a Satellite internet connection. The smart grid features automatic billing, smart scheduling, overload detection and many safety features. The local consumption data of every user are logged onto a local server by using Fiber Optic and Ethernet network. The data on the server are then analyzed automatically and the system executes the measures required if any. The scheduling feature of the smart grid allows scheduling the supply to each household depending upon their requirement. The overload detection mechanism prevents the user to connect heavy loads to the supply as the supply is limited and purely solar. The automatic billing system detects the payment made by the customer and if the customer has no dues supply is enabled for him/her. If the customer has not paid the bill the supply is automatically cut-off within 10 days. The overall IOT usage helps to keep track of overall operation of the grid as the grid is located in a remote place and is not always convenient to visit personally.

  • Client: OREDA, Odisha and , USA
  • Technologies: JAVA
  • Smart Village Award, Smart Cities Conference & Exhibition 2016
  • Coverage by DW News
